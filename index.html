Understanding Bathtub Refinishing

Bathtub refinishing, also known as bathtub reglazing or tub refinishing, is a cost-effective method to restore the surface of a worn or damaged bathtub. This process involves cleaning the tub, repairing any chips or cracks, and applying a new coating to give it a fresh, glossy finish. It's an ideal solution for homeowners looking to update their bathroom without the expense and hassle of a full replacement.
